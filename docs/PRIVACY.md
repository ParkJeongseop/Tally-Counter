# Privacy Policy — Physical Tally Counter

**Last updated: July 22, 2026**

Physical Tally Counter ("the App") is provided by PARK JEONGSEOP ("we", "us", or "our").
This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Summary

**The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.**
Everything you create in the App stays on your device.

## Information We Collect

We collect **nothing**. The App has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising,
and no third-party tracking. We do not receive any data from your device.

## Information Stored on Your Device

To make the App work, the following is saved **locally on your device only**:

- Your current count value
- Your saved counting history and any labels you enter
- Your goal setting
- Your language and app preferences

This data is stored using your device's local storage. It is never uploaded to any
server, never sent to us, and never shared with any third party. It is not accessible
to us in any form.

## Permissions

The App requests only the **vibration** permission, used to provide haptic feedback
when you count. The App does **not** request internet access and works fully offline.

## Third Parties

The App contains no third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or
crash reporting services. No data is shared with anyone.

## Deleting Your Data

Because all data is stored only on your device, you can remove it at any time by
clearing your counting history within the App, or by uninstalling the App, which
deletes all associated local data.

## Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. Since it collects no personal information from
anyone, it collects none from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on
this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
